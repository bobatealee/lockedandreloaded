This was a passion project by myself (Rev) that was generously assembled as a webpage by Boba.

This isn't meant to be seen as an official update or leak of any kind. It's just something that I wanted to make for a long time.

I wanted to create something that I thought many players, myself included, would enjoy seeing. Something to inspire fans of TF2 and make players think of the possibilities for future content.

I know I can't please everyone with what I'm showing here. Friends have given me generous feedback on what ideas they enjoyed or thought needed improvements.

Overall, I hope folks enjoy looking at this fan patch as much as I enjoyed putting it together.

Of course, I don't expect anyone at Valve to consider the ideas presented in these pages, let alone see them. This is all just for fun, and for the love of the game.

Thank you so much for reading, it's amazing to see people enjoying this game for so many years.

With love, Rev
